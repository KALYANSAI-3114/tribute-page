A.P.J. Abdul Kalam

1931-2015

Missile Man of India

Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam, often referred to as the "Missile Man of India," was a renowned Indian aerospace scientist and the 11th President of India from 2002 to 2007. He played a pivotal role in India's civilian space program and military missile development, earning him the moniker. His contributions to the country's defense technology,especially the development of ballistic missile and launch vehicle technology, were significant. Dr. Kalam authored several biographies and inspirational books, including "Wings of Fire," which chronicles his early life and career, "Ignited Minds," focusing on inspiring the youth of India, and "My Journey," detailing his personal experiences and achievements. These works offer deep insights into his vision, values, and dedication to scientific advancement and national development.
